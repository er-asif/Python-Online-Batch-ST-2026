Polymorphism means many forms.
When there same method and we do perform different functionalities then this is known as Polymorphism

# int + int = summation
# str + str = concatenation

There are two types of Polymorphism - 
    1. Method Overloading - compile time (Python do not support this directly) 
    2. Method Overriding - runtime
